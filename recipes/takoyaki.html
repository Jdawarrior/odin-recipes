Takoyaki

Description:

A small piece of octopus cooked in a thick ball of batter

Ingredients:

  • dashi-flavored batter
  • boiled octopus
  • beni shoga
  • green onioN
  • tenkasu (tempura scraps)
  • takoyaki sauce
  • garnishes (dried green seaweed, dried fish flakes)

Steps:

  1. Mix batter with water, onion and beni shoga until runny but thick.
  2. Pour oil over entire dimpled "takoyaki" pan.
  3. When oil is smoking, fill dimples completely with batter.
  4. Place octopus chunk into holes. Batter will overflow naturally.
  5. Fold overflowed batter onto octopus ball as you flip the wad 90 degrees to let it cook into a ball.
  6. Rotate balls in their dimples to brown evenly.
  7. Plate fully browned balls and top with garnishes.
